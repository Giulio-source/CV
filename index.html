Giulio Poggia

Classical pianist and Piano Teacher.

Check me out on YouTube or www.giuliopoggia.com.

I have trained in London, at Trinity Laban Conservatoire of Music and Dance, one of the top music schools in the UK.

I have many years of experience in teaching music to all levels, from total beginner to advanced.

As a performer, I have played all over Europe both as a solo player and in chamber music ensembles and last year
I have had the amazing opportunity to record at the Wigmore Hall, a legendary concert hall here in London.
